Groupenvy

Groupenvy is free and easy to use group/event sharing app.

Search, create, share, join, promote and interact with your group of friends and pretty much everyone else.

Search, explore new events and specials going on in your neighbourhood city and state and every venue across the world.

Create a new group or event on Groupenvy and share it on Facebook, Twitter and Email.

Connect with people based on things you're into and be part of a social community.
